# llm-skills

## cv-tailor

Tailors your CV to a specific job offer. Provide a job listing (text, URL, screenshot, or PDF) and the skill adapts your resume YAML to match — reordering, rewriting, and emphasizing the most relevant experience — then renders a one-page PDF.

**Requires setup before use:** wire your own background knowledge bases and photo as described in [`cv-tailor/SKILL.md`](cv-tailor/SKILL.md).
